Welcome to Crypto World

Encrypt, decrypt and break different types of decrypted messages

Goal

Our goal is to help students who are currently learning or interested in cryptography.

What it does

  • Handle user-inputs resulting in decrypted or encrypted messages
  • Encryption and decryption
  • Select different ciphers such as Caesar cipher, Vigenere cipher, Playfair cipher, etc.
  • Take in required elements for certain ciphers. For instance, Caesar cipher requires a key between 0-25 that represents a letter.
